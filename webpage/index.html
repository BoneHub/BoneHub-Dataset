BoneHub Dataset

A comprehensive, harmonized dataset for 3D bone shapes, medical images, and related metadata, generated from public datasets, designed for biomedical research.

This dataset is a work in progress and will be updated regularly. If you have any questions, suggestions, or would like to contribute, please feel free to reach out to us via email.

📖 About

This dataset contains 3D bone shapes derived from public CT and MRI datasets, along with associated metadata.
